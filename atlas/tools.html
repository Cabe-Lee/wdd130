Tools Tutorial

Here are things you must know about building with your tools.

Snippers: Easily cut the plastic runners with cleaner cuts for the parts to assemble. The models come in plastic runners (plastic rigs inside the package box.) and need to be separated from them. In order to have finer quality models, you would need to have these snippers to make more cleaner cuts off the parts of the runners.

Gundam markers: As you build the kit, the parts need some touch up. With these parts and these tools at your disposal, you can make the models appear with more color like brighter or darker or muddy or even snowed on depending how you want the model to appear. Like for example, the Gundam Ground Type is an excellent model to have a muddy and possibly damaged weathering look.

Filer: Helps to have a better way to do two things. One, you can clean the parts from plastic nibs (plastic cut residue.) and smooth it out and appear of higher quality to the model itself.

Each one of these tools are used very differently but achieve the same purpose for that nice finish.
